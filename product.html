Frequently Asked Questions

What if my cat won't use it?+

Most cats take to the wheel within a few days using the free training guide. But if yours truly won't — for any reason — you have a full 90 days to send it back for a complete refund, return shipping on us. The risk is entirely ours.

Is it noisy? I have anxious cats.+

No. Zoomi runs on precision roller bearings engineered to be near-silent, with no wobble. That quiet, stable motion is exactly why nervous cats are willing to step on in the first place.

How big is it, and will my cat fit?+

The wheel is a full 40" (about 100 cm tall) — large enough for a natural, spine-safe stride. It comfortably handles cats up to 30 lbs, including Maine Coons and other big breeds, and works great for kittens too.

How hard is it to assemble?+

Easy — it arrives flat and goes together in about 20 minutes with clear step-by-step instructions and minimal tools.

Will it scratch my floors or move around?+

No. The wide base includes anti-slip pads that keep it planted and protect hardwood and tile, even when your cat sprints.

How fast does it ship?+

Free shipping, dispatched within 1–2 business days from our US warehouse, typically delivered in 3–6 days.
