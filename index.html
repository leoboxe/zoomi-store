Before you buy

What if my cat won't use it?
Most cats take to the wheel within a few days using our free training guide. But if yours truly won't — for any reason — you have a full 90 days to send it back for a complete refund. The risk is on us, not you.
Is it noisy? I have anxious cats.
No. Zoomi runs on precision roller bearings designed to be near-silent, with no wobble. That quiet, stable motion is exactly why nervous cats are willing to step on in the first place.
How big is it, and will my cat fit?
The wheel is a full 40" (about 100 cm tall) — large enough for a natural, spine-safe stride. It comfortably handles cats up to 30 lbs, including Maine Coons and other big breeds.
Is it hard to assemble?
No — it arrives flat and goes together in about 20 minutes with clear step-by-step instructions and minimal tools.
What does shipping cost?
Shipping is completely free within the US, and so are returns. Most orders arrive within 3–6 business days.
